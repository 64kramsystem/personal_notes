## IEEE754

Precision bits (sgn, exp, mant)

- Single precision bits: 1 + 8  + 23
- Double precision bits: 1 + 11 + 52

Exponent: signed
Mantissa: 1.0 + 0.5^(left position 1-based); there are special cases
